Chocolate Lava Cake

Preparation time: 15 minutes | Cooking time: 12 minutes | Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 113g (1/2 cup) unsalted butter
  • 113g (4 oz) high-quality dark chocolate
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 6 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter and cocoa powder for ramekins
  • Vanilla ice cream or whipped cream (for serving)
  • Fresh berries (optional, for garnish)
  • Powdered sugar (for dusting)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Butter four 6-ounce ramekins and dust with cocoa powder.
  2. Place the butter and chocolate in a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring after each, until melted and smooth. Alternatively, melt in a double boiler.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, whole eggs, egg yolks, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  4. Pour the melted chocolate mixture into the egg mixture and stir to combine.
  5. Gently fold in the flour and salt until just combined. Don't overmix.
  6. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared ramekins.
  7. Place the ramekins on a baking sheet and bake for 12-14 minutes until the edges are firm but the center is still soft.
  8. Remove from the oven and let sit for 1 minute. Run a knife around the edges to loosen.
  9. Carefully invert each cake onto a serving plate. Let stand for about 10 seconds before lifting off the ramekin.
  10. Dust with powdered sugar, garnish with fresh berries if desired, and serve immediately with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.
