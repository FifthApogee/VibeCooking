Gallo Pinto

Preparation time: 10 minutes | Cooking time: 15 minutes | Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1/4 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 green or red bell pepper, diced
  • 3 cups cooked red or black beans
  • 3 cups cooked white rice
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro (culantro), chopped

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large skillet and saut√© the onion and bell pepper until soft.
  2. Add the well-drained beans and fry for three to five minutes. Add a little bean cooking broth if you see they are getting too dry.
  3. Incorporate the rice and continue frying for about three more minutes, adding a little more bean broth so the gallo pinto doesn't get too dry. Finally, garnish with chopped cilantro.
