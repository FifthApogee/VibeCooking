Hagelslag Broodje

Preparation time: 2 minutes | Cooking time: 0 minutes | Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of bread (traditionally white bread)
  • Butter, room temperature
  • Hagelslag sprinkles (chocolate sprinkles)

Instructions

  1. First, get a slice of bread. Per tradition it should be plain white bread, but you can use whatever kind of bread you'd like.
  2. Next, slather butter on your bread. A thick layer of butter is recommended. It helps if your butter is softened to room temperature before spreading it on the bread.
  3. Now, cover your bread in hagelslag (chocolate sprinkles). Milk chocolate sprinkles are popular, but you can use dark chocolate if you prefer.
